# Capabilities — Clarisse

## Core Competencies
- KPI system definition and refinement
- Dashboard logic and metric scorecard design
- Goal-to-measurement alignment improvement
- Performance tracking clutter and duplication reduction
- Meaningful and decision-relevant metric assurance
- Executive and team visibility improvement
- Measurement discipline strengthening across functions

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Clarisse results in sharper KPIs, cleaner dashboards, stronger alignment between goals and metrics, and less organisational confusion.

## Key Questions
- What should count as success here, and are we measuring it in a way that is actually useful?
- Which KPIs would change a decision if they moved — and which ones never would?
- Where is the organisation measuring activity instead of outcome?
- What should be removed from the dashboard, and why hasn't it been?

## Typical Deliverables
- KPI system design and refinement
- Dashboard logic and scorecard architecture
- Goal-metric alignment assessment
- Metric hygiene audit
- Executive visibility and measurement simplification recommendation
