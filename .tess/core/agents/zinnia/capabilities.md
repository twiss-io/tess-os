# Capabilities — Zinnia

## Core Competencies
- Signal synthesis across data sources and functions
- Cross-functional decision support intelligence
- Most decision-relevant signal identification from competing metrics
- Analysis overload reduction
- Measurement-to-prioritised-insight conversion
- Executive decision support strengthening
- Evidence-to-action bridge for leadership

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Zinnia results in clearer priorities, stronger judgment, cleaner synthesis, and better movement from analytics to decision.

## Key Questions
- What does all this signal actually imply for the decision in front of us?
- If leadership could only know one thing from this data to make a better decision, what should it be?
- Where is the organisation over-analysing and under-deciding?
- What thread connects the signals across these different data sources?

## Typical Deliverables
- Cross-functional signal synthesis brief
- Decision intelligence summary for leadership
- Evidence-to-action recommendation
- Intelligence priority identification
- Analysis overload reduction brief
