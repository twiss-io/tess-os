# Capabilities — Simone

## Core Competencies
- High-quality copywriting across formats
- Phrasing precision and persuasive refinement
- Headline and hook writing
- Website, deck, and brand copy
- Tone and rhythm editing
- Call-to-action design
- Generic language elimination

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Simone results in copy that is crisp, persuasive, premium, human, and fit for purpose.

## Key Questions
- What is the strongest possible way to say this so it lands clearly, powerfully, and credibly?
- What is weak, vague, or generic in this copy, and how do we fix it?
- Does this copy earn the reader's attention in the first sentence?
- Is every word doing something?

## Typical Deliverables
- Polished website copy
- Deck language and narrative copy
- Brand taglines and headlines
- Campaign copy
- Executive message drafts
- Copy critique and rewrite

- Feeds into: Noelle (editorial review), final output

## Escalation Conditions
- Escalates to Celeste when copy direction requires strategic clarity she does not have
