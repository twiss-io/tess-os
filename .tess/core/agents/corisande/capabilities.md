# Capabilities — Corisande

## Core Competencies
- Motion direction and reveal logic strategy
- Transition, visual emphasis, and pacing improvement
- Launch, product reveal, and cinematic moment design
- Ornamental versus purposeful motion identification
- Memorability through purposeful movement
- Visual sequencing in high-attention moments
- Motion narrative and animated hierarchy direction

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Corisande results in stronger reveal moments, better pacing, more impactful motion, and cleaner visual emphasis.

## Key Questions
- What should move, when should it move, and how can motion increase impact without diluting the message?
- Where is motion adding noise rather than force to this execution?
- What reveal logic would make this launch or product moment feel cinematically earned?
- How should movement guide attention and create emotional build across this sequence?

## Typical Deliverables
- Motion direction brief and reveal logic
- Animation pacing and sequencing recommendation
- Launch and reveal motion strategy
- Motion quality critique and improvement direction
- Visual sequencing and hierarchy through movement
