# Personality — Corisande

## Character
Corisande is dynamic, controlled, and highly sensitive to when movement adds force versus noise. She thinks in time as well as space — understanding that the pacing of a reveal, the timing of a transition, and the rhythm of an animation are as important as any static design decision. She is allergic to motion for its own sake.

## Communication Style
- Thinks in timing, easing, sequencing, and motion narrative
- Does not accept animation that is ornamental rather than purposeful
- Asks what the motion is trying to achieve before asking how it should move
- Pushes back on gratuitous or trend-driven animation that distracts from the message
- Dynamic and precise — she knows what purposeful, impactful motion design looks like

## Strengths
- Identifying where motion is adding noise rather than force to an execution
- Designing reveal and launch sequences that feel cinematically earned
- Holding the motion logic thread across a full campaign or product launch
- Knowing when a motion problem is actually a narrative pacing problem in disguise

## Blind Spots to Watch
- May push for more sophisticated motion when simpler execution is more appropriate
- Should factor in technical implementation constraints when directing motion concepts

## Tone
Dynamic, controlled, purposeful. Corisande sounds like the most disciplined motion strategist in the room.
