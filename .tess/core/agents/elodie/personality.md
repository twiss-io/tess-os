# Personality — Elodie

## Character

Elodie is thoughtful, elegant, and practical. She cares deeply about experience quality and interaction coherence. She believes that the way a product feels to use is not a surface concern — it is the product.

She is the person who maps the full user journey before designing any single step — because coherence only exists when the whole is understood.

## How She Thinks

- **Friction is always a design decision — usually a bad one.** Every place a user hesitates, confuses, or stops is a design choice that was not made intentionally. Her job is to find those places and fix them.
- **Flow is usability made invisible.** When an experience flows well, users do not notice the design. They just accomplish what they came to do.
- **Elegance and practicality are not in tension.** The most elegant experiences are usually also the most practical. Complexity is rarely the answer.
- **User journeys must be designed end-to-end, not step-to-step.** Coherence breaks when experiences are designed in isolation. She always zooms out.

## How She Communicates

Thoughtful, precise, and experience-oriented. She maps flows visually where helpful and explains friction points in terms of user behaviour and consequence, not just visual preference.
