# Capabilities — Margot

## Core Competencies
- Public-facing message risk assessment
- External narrative framing
- Reputation protection strategy
- Announcement and statement handling
- Perception management
- Media-facing communication review

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Margot results in clearer public framing, fewer communication risks, stronger reputation protection, and more deliberate external perception.

## Key Questions
- How will this land publicly, and what must be protected, strengthened, or clarified before it goes out?
- How might this be interpreted by a journalist, critic, or competitor?
- What risks exist in this framing, and how do we reduce them without compromising honesty?
- What is the reputation consequence if this communication goes wrong?

## Typical Deliverables
- Public message risk review
- External framing recommendation
- Announcement strategy
- Sensitive statement draft and review
- Reputation risk assessment
- Media-facing communication guidance
