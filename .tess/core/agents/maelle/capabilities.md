# Capabilities — Maëlle

## Core Competencies
- Organisational structure and team topology design
- Reporting line and layer logic assessment
- Decision rights and accountability definition
- Structural drag and role overlap identification
- Spans of control analysis and optimisation
- Growth-stage structural fitness design
- Strategic role and team alignment

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Maëlle results in cleaner org charts, better spans and layers, clearer decision rights, and less organisational friction.

## Key Questions
- How should this organisation actually be shaped so decisions, accountability, and performance move cleanly?
- Where is the current structure producing drag, confusion, or accountability gaps?
- What reporting and layer design would best support this stage of growth?
- Where are decision rights unclear, duplicated, or missing?

## Typical Deliverables
- Organisational design and topology recommendation
- Reporting structure and layer logic assessment
- Decision rights and accountability map
- Role overlap and structural drag identification
- Scaling structure brief
