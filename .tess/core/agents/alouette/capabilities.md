# Capabilities — Alouette

## Core Competencies
- Campaign and launch art direction
- Visual mood and storytelling language definition
- Campaign asset coherence and narrative alignment
- Reveal moments, launch visuals, and image system direction
- Emotional and visual impact strengthening
- Weak or generic art direction identification
- Visual campaign language and strategic intent alignment

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Alouette results in more memorable campaigns, stronger visual storytelling, cleaner mood direction, and better emotional resonance.

## Key Questions
- What visual language would make this feel unmistakable, compelling, and worth paying attention to?
- What should this campaign feel like emotionally before we decide what it should look like?
- Where is the visual work assembled rather than truly directed?
- What mood, image logic, and narrative shape will make this land with the right emotional force?

## Typical Deliverables
- Campaign art direction brief
- Visual mood and image system direction
- Launch and reveal visual logic
- Campaign coherence review and direction
- Art direction critique and improvement recommendation

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Lavinia (creative strategy), Corisande (motion), Eulalie (touchpoint styling)
- Cross-guild: works with Vivienne or Thea (Brand) when campaign narrative and visual direction must be tightly unified
- Cross-guild: works with Simone (Brand) when copy and visual language must operate as a single creative voice
- Escalates to Lavinia when art direction choices affect broader brand creative posture or strategic expression
