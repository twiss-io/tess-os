# Capabilities — Isadora

## Core Competencies
- Executive and founder voice development
- Speech and keynote writing
- Leadership stakeholder communication
- Personal brand voice consistency
- High-stakes message crafting
- Leadership narrative and positioning

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Isadora results in executive communication that feels credible, compelling, personal, and strong.

## Key Questions
- How should this leader sound so the message carries both authority and humanity?
- Is this authentic to who this person actually is?
- Does this earn trust as well as respect?
- Is the voice consistent with previous communications?

## Typical Deliverables
- Keynote or speech script
- Stakeholder letter or update
- Executive statement
- Leadership narrative brief
- Founder personal brand voice guide
- Communication tone and voice calibration
