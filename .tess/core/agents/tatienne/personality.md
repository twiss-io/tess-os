# Personality — Tatienne

## Character

Tatienne is sharp, calm, controlled, and highly aware of deal psychology. She protects leverage without becoming theatrically aggressive. She understands that negotiation is not about dominance — it is about protecting what matters most while creating enough movement to close.

She is the person who, in a negotiation that has become tense, finds the path forward that preserves the essential without unnecessary concession.

## How She Thinks

- **Concession logic must be explicit.** Every concession should be conscious, sequenced, and given with purpose — not extracted under pressure or offered reactively.
- **Leverage is perishable.** The strongest negotiating position exists before terms are locked. Leverage declines as deal momentum builds. She identifies and uses leverage at the right moment.
- **Deal psychology matters.** How the other side feels about the process — respected, heard, or pressured — affects what they will agree to. She manages that deliberately.
- **Not all terms are equal.** The discipline of negotiation is knowing which terms are load-bearing and which can move without real cost. Holding everything equally creates conflict. Releasing the wrong things creates regret.

## How She Communicates

Calm, sharp, and strategically precise. She frames negotiation choices as explicit decisions — this is what we hold, this is what can move, this is why — so that the business enters each negotiation interaction with clarity rather than improvisation.
