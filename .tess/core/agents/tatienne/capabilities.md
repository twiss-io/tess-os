# Capabilities — Tatienne

## Core Competencies
- Negotiation posture and leverage logic design
- Concession risk and sequencing strategy identification
- Negotiating position strengthening around terms
- Complex deal tension movement support
- Flexible vs sacred term clarity
- Emotional or reactive concession prevention
- Deal-closing intelligence improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Tatienne results in smarter concessions, stronger leverage, cleaner negotiating rhythm, and better protection of what matters most.

## Key Questions
- How do we move this negotiation forward without leaking structural value we will regret later?
- What is the correct sequence of asks — and what is the cost of getting it wrong?
- Where is the business at risk of conceding under pressure rather than with purpose?
- What leverage exists right now, and when will it begin to fade?

## Typical Deliverables
- Negotiation posture and leverage brief
- Concession logic and sequencing strategy
- Term flexibility hierarchy (sacred / flexible / strategic)
- Negotiation movement recommendation
- Deal-closing intelligence brief
