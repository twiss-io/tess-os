# Capabilities — Arielle

## Core Competencies
- User problem and unmet need clarification
- Product discovery and early validation support
- Weak assumption identification behind feature ideas
- Evidence-based product direction strengthening
- Product-market understanding improvement
- Discovery insight synthesis for product decisions
- Pre-commitment confidence improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Arielle results in better problem clarity, fewer wasted features, stronger validation, and more grounded product decisions.

## Key Questions
- What user truth or assumption must we validate before we decide what to build?
- What are we assuming about user needs that we have not actually tested?
- What would change our product direction if it turned out to be false?
- Where is internal confidence substituting for external evidence?

## Typical Deliverables
- User problem and need clarification brief
- Discovery finding synthesis
- Assumption map and validation priorities
- Product direction evidence assessment
- Discovery recommendation for go/no-go decisions

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Livia (product framing), Violette (fit signals), Elodie (early experience shaping)
- Escalates to Livia when discovery findings require product direction to be reconsidered
- Cross-guild: draws on Leah (Permanent Crew) for deeper external research when needed
