# Capabilities — Callista

## Core Competencies
- Premium service and hospitality moment design
- Experiential detail and touchpoint improvement
- Emotional polish and consistency in service delivery
- Flat, cold, or transactional journey moment identification
- Premium perception strengthening through experience design
- Sensory, emotional, and relational quality improvement
- Memorable and brand-aligned customer moment creation

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Callista results in more premium-feeling journeys, stronger emotional resonance, better service polish, and more memorable customer experiences.

## Key Questions
- What details or moments would make this experience feel genuinely cared-for, elevated, and worthy of the brand promise?
- Where does the customer journey feel functional but forgettable — and what would make it memorable?
- What touchpoints are communicating the wrong thing about the brand through their quality or tone?
- What would a genuinely hospitality-minded organisation do differently at this moment in the journey?

## Typical Deliverables
- Premium service and hospitality moment design brief
- Touchpoint emotional polish and detail improvement plan
- Journey elevation assessment and recommendation
- Brand-aligned customer moment design
- Premium experience consistency audit and redesign

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Evangeline (experience strategy), Cressida (onboarding), Yselle (trust repair)
- Cross-guild: works with Celeste or Vivienne (Brand) when brand-expression coherence matters
- Cross-guild: works with Amara or Sofia (Ops) when experience improvements require operational implementation
- Escalates to Evangeline when premium experience design raises broader brand positioning or trust questions
