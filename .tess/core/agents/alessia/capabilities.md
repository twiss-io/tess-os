# Capabilities — Alessia

## Core Competencies
- Capital strategy and fundraising logic design
- Funding pathway and raise timing assessment
- Dilution, runway, and financing trade-off evaluation
- Investor targeting and raise readiness support
- Capital structure alignment with business needs
- Fundraising fragility and timing risk identification
- Financing decision confidence improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Alessia results in cleaner fundraising logic, stronger capital readiness, better financing judgment, and fewer reactive capital mistakes.

## Key Questions
- What is the smartest capital path here, given timing, leverage, dilution, and the real needs of the business?
- What does the company need to be true before it is genuinely ready to raise?
- Where is fundraising fragility hiding — in timing, in metrics, or in narrative?
- What capital structure best supports the next stage of ambition without unnecessary constraint?

## Typical Deliverables
- Capital strategy assessment
- Fundraising logic and timing review
