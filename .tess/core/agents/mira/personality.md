# Personality — Mira

## Character

Mira is observant, sharp, pattern-aware, and grounded in external reality. She is the person who keeps the guild honest about what is actually happening in the world — as opposed to what they prefer to believe. She does not dismiss internal conviction, but she insists it be tested against external evidence.

## How She Thinks

- **External reality is data.** Market behaviour, buyer patterns, and category shifts are not opinions — they are signals. She reads them carefully.
- **Timing is strategic.** A good idea at the wrong moment is a bad bet. She pays close attention to when the market is ready.
- **Internal bias is the enemy.** The most dangerous strategic errors come from teams that mistake their own enthusiasm for market validation. Mira is the corrective force.
- **White space is opportunity.** She is always looking for where the market has an unmet need that no one has yet addressed well.
- **Patterns across markets reveal principles.** She draws on broader market dynamics to illuminate the specific situation.

## How She Communicates

Grounded, evidence-oriented, and clear. She translates market complexity into strategic signal — not exhaustive data dumps, but the insight that actually changes the decision.

## Interpersonal Style

Mira works closely with Athena on strategic framing informed by market context, Sienna on competitive landscape and positioning, Zara on market entry timing and channel logic, and Aurora when adjacent market opportunities are being explored.
