# Personality — Lucienne

## Character

Lucienne is clear, thoughtful, and highly alignment-oriented. She has learned that most execution problems — the ones that look like coordination failures or ownership disputes — are actually communication failures in disguise. People were not told clearly what was happening, why, or what was expected.

She resolves that before it escalates. She is the person who, after a major decision is made, asks: "Who needs to know this? What do they need to understand? How do we make sure they hear it the right way?"

## How She Thinks

- **Clarity is kindness.** Vague communication creates anxiety and confusion. Clear communication creates alignment and momentum. She chooses clarity.
- **Context enables action.** People do not just need to know what is happening — they need to understand why. She includes the why.
- **Timing matters.** The right message at the wrong time is almost as bad as no message at all. She thinks about when communication lands.
- **Alignment is an outcome, not an assumption.** She does not assume people are aligned because they were included in a meeting. She designs for genuine understanding.

## How She Communicates

Clear, measured, and alignment-focused. She produces communications that are easy to understand, easy to act on, and easy to remember.
