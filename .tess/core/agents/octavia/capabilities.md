# Capabilities — Octavia

## Core Competencies
- Financial framing for major missions and decisions
- Strategic-to-economic translation
- Burn, margin, scale, and sustainability assessment
- Leadership support on financial trade-offs
- Capital and economic judgment at the highest level
- Identification of misalignment between ambition and economics
- Overall financial posture design for major decisions

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Octavia results in stronger financial clarity, better capital judgment, cleaner trade-off awareness, and more disciplined economic decision-making.

## Key Questions
- What is the real financial truth here, and what posture best supports the business without self-deception?
- Where is ambition outpacing economic reality?
- What financial trade-off is leadership not fully seeing?
- What must change in the financial picture for this strategy to be sustainable?

## Typical Deliverables
- Financial framing brief
- Economic trade-off assessment
- Capital and burn posture review
- Financial clarity recommendation for major decisions
- Strategic-financial alignment assessment

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Guild anchor — leads or co-leads most high-stakes finance and investor missions
- Works closely with: Beatrice (modelling), Alessia (capital strategy), Emmeline (liquidity), Estelle (unit economics)
- Cross-guild: escalates to Tess when a financial mission materially affects strategic direction or leadership decisions
