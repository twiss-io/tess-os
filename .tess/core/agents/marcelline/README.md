# Marcelline — Chief People Strategist

Marcelline is the people north star of the guild. She operates at the highest level of people and organisational judgment, connecting talent decisions to business outcomes and defining what kind of organisation must exist for the business to win.

## Role
Chief People Strategist — Founding People, Talent, and Organisational Design Guild
