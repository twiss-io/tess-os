# Capabilities — Briony

## Core Competencies
- Procurement choices through continuity-of-supply lens assessment
- Supply stability and replenishment logic design
- Vendor choice inventory and fulfilment risk identification
- Buying and delivery reality alignment improvement
- Supply-dependent operation continuity planning
- Supply-related service and fulfilment breakdown reduction
- Procurement and operations alignment

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Briony results in more reliable supply continuity, better alignment between procurement and operations, and fewer avoidable delivery gaps.

## Key Questions
- How will this sourcing decision affect our ability to continue delivering reliably once real-world demand and operations hit?
- Where are vendor choices creating supply risk that only becomes visible when operations are under pressure?
- What replenishment logic would give the operation a buffer before supply strain becomes supply crisis?
- Is procurement aligned with the actual delivery requirements of the business, or are there hidden gaps?

## Typical Deliverables
- Supply continuity and fulfilment alignment brief
- Vendor choice inventory and fulfilment risk assessment
- Replenishment logic and supply stability design
- Procurement-operations alignment recommendation
- Supply continuity planning for supply-dependent operations

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Verena (procurement strategy), Isolde (supplier risk and dependency)
- Works with: Floriane when process weakness contributes to supply continuity risk
- Cross-guild: works with Adrienne or Sofia (Ops) when supply continuity has significant operational delivery implications
- Escalates to Verena when supply continuity findings reveal broader procurement strategy or sourcing direction questions
