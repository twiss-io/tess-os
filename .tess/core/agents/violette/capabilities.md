# Capabilities — Violette

## Core Competencies
- Product-market fit signal assessment
- Adoption and activation pattern interpretation
- Product value landing and resonance analysis
- User pull and repeat engagement improvement support
- Fit-related product weakness clarification
- Product traction judgment strengthening
- Product behaviour and market resonance connection

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Violette results in better product-market understanding, clearer fit signals, stronger adoption insight, and more grounded product decisions.

## Key Questions
- Is this product truly resonating, and what evidence suggests real fit versus superficial usage?
- What does the activation and retention data actually tell us about whether users find value?
- Where is internal confidence about fit diverging from what the behavior data shows?
- What would genuine product pull look like, and do we currently have it?

## Typical Deliverables
- Product-market fit assessment
- Adoption and activation analysis
- Fit signal interpretation brief
- Retention-related product insight summary
- Traction review and recommendation

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Arielle (discovery), Livia (product direction)
- Cross-guild: works with Bianca (Growth Guild) when downstream growth interpretation of fit signals matters
- Escalates to Livia when fit assessment reveals fundamental product direction or positioning issues
