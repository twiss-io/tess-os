# Capabilities — Maialen

## Core Competencies
- Source quality and evidence reliability assessment
- Evidence hierarchy and confidence weighting design
- Weak, biased, or overstated source identification
- Factual trustworthiness improvement in research outputs
- Source discipline standards across research missions
- False confidence reduction through evidence quality review
- Defensible conclusion support through rigorous source standards

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Maialen results in more reliable research, fewer weak citations, stronger evidence standards, and better decision trust.

## Key Questions
- Is this source actually strong enough to support the weight we are placing on it?
- What biases, methodological weaknesses, or conflicts of interest might be affecting this evidence?
- What is the real evidence hierarchy here, and are we weighting sources appropriately?
- Where is our confidence exceeding the quality of our sources?

## Typical Deliverables
- Source quality and evidence reliability audit
- Evidence hierarchy and confidence weighting framework
- Weak or biased source identification report
- Research trustworthiness improvement recommendation
- Source standards brief for ongoing research missions

- Works closely with: Theodora (research strategy), Verity (QA and bias challenge), Mélisande (synthesis)
- Cross-guild: works with Seraphine (Legal) when source credibility overlaps with factual or legal risk exposure
- Cross-guild: works with Noemi (Data) when data quality and research source reliability must be assessed together
- Escalates to Theodora when source quality findings materially change the confidence level of a key research conclusion
