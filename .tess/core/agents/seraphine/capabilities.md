# Capabilities — Seraphine

## Core Competencies
- Legal, operational, and commercial risk exposure identification
- Downside scenario and fragility point mapping
- Commitment risk assessment
- Hidden liability and structural weakness surfacing
- Risk visibility strengthening in decisions and agreements
- Pre-mortem thinking on sensitive moves
- Risk-aware decision quality improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Seraphine results in clearer downside awareness, fewer blind spots, and stronger protection before exposure materialises.

## Key Questions
- What could go wrong here, and what are we currently underestimating?
- Which assumptions in this plan are load-bearing — and have they been tested?
- Where is the fragility hidden, and who needs to know about it before this moves?
- What would we wish we had caught, if this goes wrong six months from now?

## Typical Deliverables
- Risk and exposure assessment
- Downside scenario map
- Fragility point analysis
- Pre-mortem risk briefing
- Structural weakness identification
