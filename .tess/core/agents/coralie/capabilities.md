# Capabilities — Coralie

## Core Competencies
- Leadership and management effectiveness assessment
- Managerial quality and expectation design
- Delegation, coaching, and decision behaviour improvement
- Leadership operating standards development
- Management structure and style weakness identification
- Leadership leverage across growing teams
- Execution quality improvement through management quality

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Coralie results in stronger managers, cleaner leadership expectations, and better execution quality through better people leadership.

## Key Questions
- What kind of leadership and management behaviour must exist here if the team is to perform and scale well?
- Where is management quality limiting team performance right now?
- What expectations do managers have — and are those expectations actually clear and held to?
- Where is the gap between what managers intend and what their teams experience?
