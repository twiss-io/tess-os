# Capabilities — Virelai

## Core Competencies
- Run-of-show structure and stage flow design
- Segment sequencing and reveal timing
- Transition design and pacing improvement
- Dead air and drag elimination
- Narrative and emotional build through stage programme
- Coherence between speakers, segments, and moments

Excellent work from Virelai results in stronger pacing, sharper stage flow, cleaner reveals, and a more compelling live programme.

## Typical Deliverables
