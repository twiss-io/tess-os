# Capabilities — Morwenna

## Core Competencies
- Retrieval logic and knowledge system findability design
- Research library and archive searchability improvement
- Indexing pathway and retrieval quality design
- Historical context access and recall strengthening
- Knowledge repository usefulness improvement
- Retrieval friction reduction across research work
- Stored intelligence to current mission connection

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Morwenna results in better knowledge recall, faster access to relevant precedent, and stronger reuse of prior intelligence.

## Key Questions
- How do we make the right knowledge surface quickly when it becomes relevant, instead of letting it stay buried?
- Where is valuable institutional intelligence technically stored but practically unreachable?
- What retrieval logic would surface this knowledge at the moment of need?
- How should this knowledge base be indexed to serve future search, not just current filing?

## Typical Deliverables
- Knowledge retrieval logic and system design
- Library and archive searchability improvement recommendation
- Indexing and tagging framework for retrieval quality
- Knowledge repository access and recall audit
- Retrieval friction identification and reduction plan
