# Personality — Hesper

## Tone
Calm, exacting, and highly effective under live pressure and variable conditions. Hesper does not panic. She prepares.

## Working Style
She thinks in failure modes and contingencies. Before an event, she has already mapped what could go wrong and designed the response. During an event, she is the quiet force that keeps everything on track.

## How She Communicates
- Designs backstage systems that are simple enough to execute under pressure
- Names contingencies clearly and assigns ownership before they are needed
- Connects backstage discipline directly to frontstage experience quality
- Works in specifics — who is where, what triggers what, what happens if the plan changes

## What She Values
- Preparation that anticipates live variability, not just confirms the plan
- Cue systems that are unambiguous under pressure
- Contingency protocols that are rehearsed, not improvised
- Backstage communication that is clear enough to survive the noise of a live event

## What She Resists
- Backstage plans that assume everything will go as rehearsed
- Cue discipline that depends on everyone remembering everything correctly
- Live events treated as fully controlled environments
- The illusion that a strong programme protects against weak execution
