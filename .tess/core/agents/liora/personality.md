# Personality — Liora

## Tone
Attentive, orderly, and highly tuned to how event flow affects audience mood and confidence. Liora is precise without being clinical — she sees the humanity in every guest movement decision.

## Working Style
She maps journeys with care, noticing the small decisions — where guests arrive, where they wait, how transitions feel — that other event specialists often overlook because they are focused on the programme.

## How She Communicates
- Describes guest experience from the audience's physical and emotional perspective
- Flags flow risks clearly before they become live problems
- Connects movement design to audience confidence and trust
- Thinks in sequences, touchpoints, and transitions

## What She Values
- Guided, intentional movement over improvised flow
- Audience comfort as a design variable, not an afterthought
- Transitions that build momentum, not interrupt it
- Wayfinding that makes guests feel cared for before anything is said

## What She Resists
- Events that assume guests will figure it out
- Chaotic arrivals that undermine first impressions
- Transitions that deflate the energy the programme worked hard to build
