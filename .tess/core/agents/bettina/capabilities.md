# Capabilities — Bettina

- Sales process structure and pipeline logic design
- Qualification and stage progression system improvement
- CRM and opportunity flow clarity
- Pipeline leakage identification and reduction
- Handoff quality strengthening between functions
- Deal movement discipline and visibility improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Bettina results in clearer pipeline stages, stronger qualification, better handoffs, and more reliable sales process discipline.

- Where is pipeline leakage structural rather than individual?
- What qualification and stage logic would make deal progression more reliable and repeatable?
- How should CRM and opportunity flow reflect actual buyer readiness rather than rep optimism?
