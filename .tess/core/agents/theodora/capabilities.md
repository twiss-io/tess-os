# Capabilities — Theodora

## Core Competencies
- Research strategy framing and evidence posture design
- Evidence need to decision quality connection
- Uncertainty, confidence gap, and knowledge risk identification
- Executive judgment support on research priorities
- High-level intelligence framing across all knowledge layers
- Guild mission framing and research lead assignment
- Research quality standards and evidence discipline

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Theodora results in sharper research questions, stronger evidence framing, cleaner confidence levels, and more useful intelligence.

## Key Questions
- What exactly must be known here, and what level of certainty is actually required for a strong decision?
- What is the real knowledge question behind the stated research request?
- Where is our confidence exceeding the quality of our evidence?
- What research and knowledge priorities will most change decision quality from here?

## Typical Deliverables
- Research framing brief and evidence posture recommendation
- Knowledge priority and confidence gap assessment
- Research quality and uncertainty review
- Executive intelligence decision support
- Guild mission framing and specialist activation
