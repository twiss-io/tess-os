# Capabilities — Mariselle

## Core Competencies
- Customer feedback and listening system design
- Review, sentiment, and experience-signal collection improvement
- VOC loop structuring and decision connection
- Internal assumption vs customer reality gap identification
- Testimonial and review readiness building
- Post-purchase listening quality strengthening
- Customer-informed decision-making improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Mariselle results in cleaner feedback systems, better visibility into customer truth, stronger testimonial pathways, and more useful experience insight.

## Key Questions
- What are customers actually telling us, what are they not saying directly, and how should that change what we do?
- Where is the gap between what we believe about customer experience and what customers are actually experiencing?
- What feedback mechanisms would produce the most actionable signal at the right moments?
- How does the current VOC system close the circle from customer insight to organisational change?

## Typical Deliverables
- Customer feedback and VOC system design
- Review and testimonial readiness brief
- Sentiment and experience-signal collection improvement plan
- Internal vs customer reality gap analysis
- VOC loop and decision-connection framework
