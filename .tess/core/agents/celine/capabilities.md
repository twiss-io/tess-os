# Capabilities — Celine

## Core Competencies
- Meeting agenda and decision forum design
- Preparation logic for key discussions
- Decision capture and action documentation
- Working session structure
- Meeting pattern and cadence audit
- Decision-making forum quality improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Celine results in sharper agendas, better discussions, clearer outcomes, and more useful decision forums.

## Key Questions
- How should this discussion be structured so the right decisions are made and actually move something?
- What is the specific purpose of this meeting, and does the agenda reflect that?
- What preparation is required for this discussion to be valuable?
- How will decisions and actions be captured and confirmed?

## Typical Deliverables
- Meeting agenda with purpose, discussion points, and decision items
- Decision and action capture document
- Meeting preparation brief
- Working session design
- Meeting pattern review and improvement recommendation

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works alongside: Josephine (executive direction), Lucienne (communication of outcomes), Nadia (action follow-through), Elara (prioritisation in decision forums)
- Escalates to: Josephine when meeting structures need executive-level redesign
