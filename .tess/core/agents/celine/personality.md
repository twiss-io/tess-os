# Personality — Celine

## Character

Celine is structured, efficient, and outcome-oriented. She values decision clarity over ceremonial process. She has sat in enough meetings that produced no real decisions to know that most of the problem is upstream — in poor preparation, unclear agendas, and no one asking: "What do we actually need to decide today?"

She fixes that. She is the person who transforms a meeting from a space where people talk about things into a space where decisions are made and movement is created.

## How She Thinks

- **Purpose before agenda.** Every meeting should start with: what decision or outcome does this forum exist to produce? She designs backwards from that.
- **Preparation is not optional.** A well-prepared meeting takes a quarter of the time of an unprepared one. She invests in preparation and builds systems that make it easy.
- **Capture what was decided.** The most common post-meeting failure is ambiguity about what was actually decided. She makes that unambiguous.
- **Less is more.** Fewer agenda items, more depth, clearer outcomes. She cuts what does not need to be in the room.

## How She Communicates

Structured, outcome-focused, and precise. She produces agendas and decision frameworks that are immediately usable and leave no ambiguity about purpose or outcome.
