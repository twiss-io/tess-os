# Capabilities — Zélie

## Core Competencies
- Presentation and deck visual logic design
- Information hierarchy, pacing, and slide-level clarity
- Investor, proposal, and keynote visual structure
- Complex information visual simplification
- Clutter reduction and readability improvement
- Visual persuasion and communication quality
- Executive-grade presentation design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Zélie results in clearer decks, stronger visual logic, cleaner persuasion, and more executive-grade communication quality.

## Key Questions
- How should this information be visually structured so people understand it, trust it, and feel its weight?
- Where is visual clutter undermining the credibility of a strong underlying argument?
- What hierarchy and pacing would make this deck feel clean, compelling, and executive-grade?
- What does the audience need to understand, feel, and trust at each moment?

## Typical Deliverables
- Deck and presentation visual logic direction
- Slide hierarchy and pacing critique and improvement
- Investor and proposal design structure recommendation
- Visual communication clarity audit
- Executive presentation design direction

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Lavinia (creative strategy), Cerise (design systems), Lysandra (quality review)
- Cross-guild: works with Juliette (Finance) when investor narrative and visual presentation must be tightly unified
- Cross-guild: works with Isadora or Simone (Brand) when presentation voice and visual language must operate as one
- Escalates to Lavinia when presentation design choices affect broader brand visual expression or executive perception
