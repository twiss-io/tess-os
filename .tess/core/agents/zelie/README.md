# Zélie — Presentation and Deck Design Specialist

Zélie is the architect of visual persuasion in structured communication. She focuses on decks, presentations, investor visuals, proposal layout, and information hierarchy — ensuring serious messages do not arrive in weak visual form.

## Role
Presentation and Deck Design Specialist — Founding Creative, Design, and Visual Systems Guild

- Visual hierarchy, pacing, and slide-level clarity improvement
- Investor and executive communication visual structure
- Turning complex information into clear, compelling visual communication
- Reducing clutter and strengthening presentation credibility
