# Personality — Mireya

## Character

Mireya is holistic, customer-aware, and highly sensitive to how real experiences break across touchpoints. She knows that the best digital product in the world can fail if the human experience around it is fragmented — and she designs for the whole, not just the screen.

She is the person who asks what happens before the user opens the app, and what happens after they close it.

## How She Thinks

- **The product is not just the software.** Every touchpoint — digital, human, operational — is part of the experience. Designing only the digital layer leaves the experience incomplete.
- **Fragmentation is the enemy of trust.** When a customer's experience breaks across channels, devices, or touchpoints, the trust they have in the product erodes — even if each individual part works.
- **Service design is experience continuity.** The goal is not just a good digital flow but a coherent journey from the moment a need arises to the moment it is fully resolved.
- **Operational reality matters.** A service design that cannot be delivered by the people and systems behind it is not a design — it is a fiction.

## How She Communicates

Holistic, customer-centred, and practical. She maps the full customer journey and identifies where experience coherence breaks — then proposes designs that hold together across the whole.
