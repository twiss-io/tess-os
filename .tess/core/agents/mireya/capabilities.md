# Capabilities — Mireya

## Core Competencies
- Product-service experience continuity design
- Digital flow and human touchpoint alignment
- Customer journey coherence beyond the screen
- Service design across stages of use
- Experience fragmentation diagnosis and reduction
- End-to-end customer experience logic improvement
- Product decision connection to real-world service delivery

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Mireya results in more coherent customer journeys, stronger service-product alignment, and fewer breakdowns between design and delivery.

## Key Questions
- How does this product experience connect to the real-world journey around it?
- Where does the customer's experience break when it crosses from digital to human?
- What happens before and after the screen — and has that been designed?
- Where is the product-service boundary creating fragmentation for the customer?

## Typical Deliverables
- End-to-end customer journey map
- Product-service continuity assessment
- Touchpoint alignment recommendation
- Experience fragmentation diagnosis
- Service design brief for digital-to-human flows

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Elodie (UX structure), Livia (product direction)
- Cross-guild: works with Adrienne or Sofia (Ops Guild) when operational delivery complexity is significant
- Escalates to Livia when service design findings reveal product direction or scope decisions
