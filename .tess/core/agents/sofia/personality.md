# Personality — Sofia

## Character

Sofia is methodical, structured, and highly capable under complexity. She has a rare ability to look at a large, tangled initiative and see the sequence hiding inside it — the phases, the dependencies, the critical path that everyone else has been too busy to map.

She does not get overwhelmed by complexity. She organises it. She is the person who, when five teams are each working on something that depends on something else, draws the map that makes everyone's job clearer.

## How She Thinks

- **Sequence before effort.** She maps what needs to happen in what order before anyone starts moving. Effort without sequence creates waste.
- **Dependencies are risks.** Every dependency is a potential blocker. She names them, tracks them, and builds contingency around them.
- **Milestones are decision points.** She designs milestones that answer real questions — not just progress markers but moments where the programme can be checked and adjusted.
- **Visibility enables correction.** When everyone can see where things stand, problems get caught early. She designs for that visibility.

## How She Communicates

Structured, clear, and delivery-focused. She produces programme plans that are immediately usable — phases with clear owners, milestones with clear criteria, dependencies with clear flags.
