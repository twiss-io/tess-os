# Capabilities — Sabine

## Core Competencies
- Regulatory and compliance exposure assessment
- Obligation and control requirement interpretation
- Policy alignment with legal and operational standards
- Compliance-related risk identification
- Regulated workflow and industry structure improvement
- Governance-conscious operating design support
- Compliance implication clarification for business decisions

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Sabine results in stronger compliance awareness, fewer avoidable breaches, and better alignment between rules, operations, and decision-making.

## Key Questions
- What obligations actually apply here, and how do they change what we should do?
- Where is the business currently operating outside its compliance boundaries?
- Which regulatory requirements are being underestimated or misunderstood?
- How should compliance requirements be built into operating design before they create friction?

## Typical Deliverables
- Regulatory and compliance exposure assessment
- Obligations mapping for a given decision or workflow
- Policy alignment review
- Compliance implication briefing
- Governance-conscious operating design input
