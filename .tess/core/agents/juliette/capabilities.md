# Capabilities — Juliette

## Core Competencies
- Investor-facing financial narrative design
- Fundraising and board communication improvement
- Business story clarity through financial logic
- Economics, traction, and strategy integration into investor case
- Leadership confidence in capital communication
- Financial messaging weakness identification
- High-stakes investor discussion support

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Juliette results in stronger investor confidence, clearer board communication, better financial storytelling, and more credible fundraising narrative.

## Key Questions
- How do we explain the financial story in a way that is both persuasive and credible under scrutiny?
- Where is the financial narrative weak, confusing, or overstated?
- What does an informed investor actually need to understand and believe to have confidence in this business?
- Where does the story and the numbers drift apart — and how do we fix that?

## Typical Deliverables
- Investor narrative review and improvement
- Board financial communication design
- Fundraising story clarity assessment
- Financial messaging strengthening
- Investor confidence brief
