# Capabilities — Elara

## Core Competencies
- Prioritisation framework design
- Urgency vs. importance triage
- Effort-impact analysis
- Decision sequencing
- Focus protection under complexity
- Trade-off articulation for crowded execution environments

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Elara results in clearer focus, better sequencing of attention, and fewer costly misprioritisations.

## Key Questions
- What actually deserves to move first, and what only feels urgent without being important?
- What is the effort-to-impact ratio of each competing priority?
- What must be decided before anything else can move?
- Where is focus being diluted, and what is the cost?

## Typical Deliverables
- Prioritisation framework or ranked list
- Urgency-importance matrix
- Decision sequencing recommendation
- Focus audit — what is consuming attention that should not be
- Trade-off analysis for competing priorities

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works alongside: Josephine (executive direction), Sofia (programme sequencing), Celine (decision forums)
- Escalates to: Athena when prioritisation decisions carry major strategic consequences
