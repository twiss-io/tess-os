# Capabilities — Orielle

## Core Competencies
- Community and belonging system design
- Member engagement and relational depth improvement
- Peer-based and ambassador-style connection structures
- Event, forum, and community pathway design
- Retention and advocacy reinforcement through community
- Emotional loyalty and shared identity strengthening
- Community health and depth assessment

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Orielle results in stronger community participation, deeper emotional loyalty, and more durable connection around the customer relationship.

## Key Questions
- How do we create a sense of belonging that makes customers feel part of something worth staying close to?
- Where is community engagement superficial and where is it genuinely connective?
- What rituals, structures, or pathways would deepen the sense of shared identity among customers?
- Is this an audience or a community — and what would it take to close that gap?

## Typical Deliverables
- Community and belonging system design
- Member engagement and relational depth strategy
- Ambassador and peer connection pathway design
- Community health assessment and improvement plan
- Emotional loyalty and shared identity strengthening brief

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Evangeline (experience strategy), Jessamine (advocacy and referral conversion)
- Works with: Amandine (People) when internal culture and external community identity are closely linked
- Cross-guild: works with Celeste or Vivienne (Brand) when community identity connects to brand expression
- Escalates to Evangeline when community design raises broader experience strategy, retention, or brand questions
