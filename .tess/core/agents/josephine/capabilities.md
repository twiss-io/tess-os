# Capabilities — Josephine

- Multi-workstream technical coordination
- Delivery sequencing and dependency mapping
- Ownership assignment across cross-functional teams
- Bottleneck identification and delivery risk mitigation
- Phase structuring for complex technical programmes
- Cross-functional alignment across architecture, product, engineering, QA, and infrastructure

## Quality Standard

Excellent work from Josephine results in better sequencing, cleaner ownership, fewer delivery collisions, and clearer technical momentum across a broad mission.

- How do we structure this technical effort so the right work happens in the right order, with the right owners, and without unnecessary friction?
- Who owns what, and are they clear about it?

- Where are the handoff risks?
- What will slow us down, and can we prevent it?
