# Capabilities — Rosalie

## Core Competencies
- Budgeting and allocation logic design
- Spend priority and investment trade-off assessment
- Budget discipline improvement across teams and initiatives
- Capital deployment decision support
- Waste, overspend, and weak allocation identification
- Resource prioritisation under constraint
- Financial priority clarity under competing demands

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Rosalie results in smarter resource deployment, clearer budgeting logic, and stronger discipline around financial priorities.

## Key Questions
- Given limited capital, where should money actually go to create the strongest return or strategic leverage?
- Where is spend disconnected from strategy, and what is the opportunity cost?
- What does this year's budget reflect — last year's inertia or this year's priorities?
- Where is capital being absorbed without clear accountability or outcome?

## Typical Deliverables
- Budgeting logic and allocation framework
- Spend priority assessment
- Investment trade-off analysis
- Resource allocation recommendation under constraint
- Budget discipline review
