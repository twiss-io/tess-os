# Capabilities — Romilly

## Core Competencies
- Diligence focus area definition
- Transaction red flag and hidden liability identification
- Risk concentration and structural fragility assessment
- Pre-deal confidence improvement through validation
- Diligence sequencing and issue prioritisation
- Downside awareness strengthening before commitment
- Post-signing surprise reduction

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Romilly results in better diligence focus, earlier risk detection, fewer hidden surprises, and stronger transaction discipline.

## Key Questions
- What do we still not know that could materially change whether or how this deal should proceed?
- Which diligence areas carry the highest risk of revealing deal-changing information — and have they been prioritised?
- Where is the confidence level being expressed in this transaction higher than the understanding actually warrants?
- What does the other side know that we have not yet been shown?

## Typical Deliverables
- Diligence priority and focus brief
- Transaction red flag and hidden liability identification
- Risk concentration and fragility assessment
- Diligence gap analysis
- Pre-commitment confidence assessment
