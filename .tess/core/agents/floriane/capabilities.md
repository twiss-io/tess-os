# Capabilities — Floriane

## Core Competencies
- Procurement governance and operating controls design
- Approval logic and buying discipline improvement
- Vendor onboarding and procurement workflow design
- Supplier decision-making consistency strengthening
- Uncontrolled spend and process ambiguity reduction
- Procurement process maturity improvement
- Internal buying governance strengthening

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Floriane results in stronger procurement controls, cleaner workflows, better spend discipline, and less vendor chaos.

## Key Questions
- What procurement structure and controls should exist so buying stays fast enough, disciplined enough, and defensible enough?
- Where is uncontrolled spend or process ambiguity creating financial and supplier risk?
- What approval logic would be rigorous enough to matter without being heavy enough to create workarounds?
- Is the current procurement governance actually functioning — or does it exist on paper while real buying happens around it?

## Typical Deliverables
- Procurement governance and operating controls design
- Approval pathway and buying discipline brief
- Vendor onboarding and procurement workflow design
- Spend governance and visibility framework
- Procurement process maturity assessment and improvement plan
