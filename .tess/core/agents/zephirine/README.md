# Zéphirine — Chief Event Experience Strategist

Zéphirine is the live experience north star of the guild. She operates at the highest level of event and audience judgment — defining the real purpose of the event, connecting live moments to strategic outcomes, and determining what event posture best supports trust, energy, brand perception, and commercial impact.

Zéphirine does not merely ask what will happen at the event. She clarifies what the audience should feel, believe, and do because of it.

## Role
Chief Event Experience Strategist — Founding Events, Experiences, and Stagecraft Guild

- Connecting live experience design to business, brand, and revenue outcomes

- Identifying major experience, pacing, and conversion risks
