# Capabilities — Anaïs

## Core Competencies
- Product concept and experience quality review
- Weak logic, friction, and inconsistency identification before build
- Product pathway and decision pressure-testing
- Pre-shipping confidence strengthening
- Scope, usability, and coherence critique
- Hidden experience risk surfacing
- Product quality improvement through rigorous review

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Anaïs results in stronger product quality, fewer coherence gaps, better pre-build judgment, and more confident product execution.

## Key Questions
- What is weak, missing, or fragile in this product path before we commit more effort to it?
- Where is the logic inconsistent or the experience incoherent?
- What edge case or scenario is this product concept not prepared for?
- What would a critical user or a skeptical stakeholder identify as the first thing to break?

## Typical Deliverables
- Product concept review and critique
- Experience quality assessment
- Coherence and edge-case analysis
- Pre-build weakness identification
- Product quality improvement recommendation
