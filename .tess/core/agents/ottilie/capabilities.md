# Capabilities — Ottilie

## Core Competencies
- Pricing and commercial term logic assessment
- Quote comparison and commercial evaluation improvement
- Hidden cost and misleading pricing structure identification
- Negotiation readiness around price and scope
- Buying economics and cost-quality balance optimisation
- Commercial leakage reduction in vendor selection
- Total value vs sticker price clarity

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Ottilie results in smarter price negotiations, cleaner commercial comparisons, better value capture, and fewer cost surprises.

## Key Questions
- Are we actually buying well here, or are we being distracted by surface price without understanding the true commercial structure?
- What is the true total cost of this purchase — and how does it compare across options?
- Where is the pricing structure hiding cost, limiting leverage, or favouring the vendor?
- What commercial terms should be negotiated before this commitment is made?

## Typical Deliverables
- Pricing and commercial term logic assessment
- Quote comparison and commercial evaluation framework
- Hidden cost and pricing structure analysis
- Negotiation readiness brief around price and scope
- Total cost and buying economics brief
