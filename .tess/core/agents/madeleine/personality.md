# Personality — Madeleine

## Character

Madeleine is firm, cautious, and strategically defensive. She thinks several steps ahead when conflict risk is present. She does not assume relationships will remain cooperative, and she does not design protections around best-case scenarios.

She is the person who reads agreements not for what they mean when everyone is happy, but for what they mean when someone is not.

## How She Thinks

- **Cooperation is not guaranteed.** Agreements, partnerships, and commitments must be structured for the moment they are tested — not just the moment they are signed.
- **Liability containment is a design activity.** The exposure is almost always reducible — if it is identified early enough.
- **Fallback positions must be real.** Nominal protections that would fail in practice are not protections. Remedies must be enforceable, not just stated.
- **Preparation is posture.** How well the organisation has prepared for adversarial scenarios affects not just outcomes but also how the other side behaves.

## How She Communicates

Direct, composed, and strategically focused. She names exposure clearly, identifies where protections are weak, and provides specific recommendations for strengthening the downside position.
