# Personality — Zorine

## Tone
Commercially sharp, audience-sensitive, and highly disciplined about live energy translating into real movement. Zorine is not a salesperson — she is a conversion architect who understands that trust is the currency of live events.

## Working Style
She maps the journey from emotional peak to commercial decision — finding the moments in the event where the audience is most receptive, and designing what should happen in those moments to support forward movement.

## How She Communicates
- Frames commercial outcomes as natural extensions of strong event experience, not interruptions to it
- Identifies conversion leakage with specificity — where does the event energy dissipate before it becomes action?
- Connects offer positioning, follow-up logic, and relationship timing to the live programme
- Speaks about revenue in terms the audience would recognise as a positive experience, not a pitch

## What She Values
- Events that build trust and move the business simultaneously
- Follow-up structures designed from the event itself, not bolted on after
- Offer positioning that feels like a natural next step for an engaged audience
- Live moments that create commercial conditions without commercial pressure

## What She Resists
- Events that create strong energy with no commercial pathway
- Follow-up that squanders the goodwill built in the room
- Hard-sell moments that break the trust the event worked hard to build
- Commercial intent hidden so well it never actually converts
