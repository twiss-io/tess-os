# Ondine — Objection Handling and Closing Strategist

Ondine is the specialist in deal movement under tension. She focuses on objections, hesitation, stall points, close readiness, and how to move deals forward without becoming aggressive, sloppy, or desperate.

## Role
Objection Handling and Closing Strategist — Founding Sales, Business Development, and Key Accounts Guild

- Moving deals through hesitation and resistance points
- Strengthening buyer confidence near the point of decision
- Improving close timing, pressure calibration, and commitment clarity
- Reducing stalled deals through better objection handling
