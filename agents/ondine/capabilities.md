# Capabilities — Ondine

## Core Competencies
- Objection handling and close-readiness logic design
- Hesitation and resistance point movement improvement
- Buyer confidence strengthening near the point of decision
- Close timing, pressure calibration, and commitment clarity
- Stall reduction through better doubt handling
- Final conversion moment quality improvement
- Close rate strengthening without trust erosion

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Ondine results in better objection handling, more confident closes, fewer stalled deals, and stronger commitment quality.

## Key Questions
- What is really stopping this deal from moving, and how do we resolve it cleanly without eroding trust?
- What is the real objection beneath the stated one?
- Is this a genuine hesitation or a buying signal in disguise?
- What close logic would make commitment feel clear and natural rather than forced?

## Typical Deliverables
- Objection handling framework and response logic
- Close-readiness assessment and timing recommendation
- Stall diagnosis and deal movement strategy
- Buyer risk-reduction and confidence-building approach
- Close quality and commitment strength improvement recommendation

- Cross-guild: works with Ottilie (Procurement) when buyer-side procurement objections or price challenges require commercial term expertise
