# Capabilities — Noemi

## Core Competencies
- Data quality and reporting integrity assessment
- Broken definition, gap, and inconsistency identification
- Reporting reliability confidence improvement
- Metric consistency across teams and tools
- Analytics trust issue surfacing
- Decision risk reduction from bad or unstable data
- Analytical defensibility strengthening across functions

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Noemi results in stronger trust in reporting, fewer silent integrity issues, better metric consistency, and more reliable analytical decisions.

## Key Questions
- Can we actually trust this data enough to make decisions from it, and if not, where is it breaking?
- Where are metrics defined differently across teams or tools — and which definition is right?
- What silent failures in the data pipeline are currently being missed?
- Where would a data integrity audit be most likely to find something important?

## Typical Deliverables
- Data quality and integrity assessment
- Broken definition and inconsistency identification
- Metric consistency audit
- Reporting reliability confidence brief
- Data trust risk summary
