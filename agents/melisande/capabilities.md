# Capabilities — Mélisande

## Core Competencies
- Complex research synthesis and insight distillation
- Large information space signal extraction
- Multiple perspective and evidence stream reconciliation
- Research overload reduction without nuance loss
- High-quality strategic synthesis
- Research usability improvement for leaders and specialists
- Research volume to decision-useful clarity conversion

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Mélisande results in stronger synthesis, clearer key takeaways, less overwhelm, and more actionable intelligence.

## Key Questions
- What truly matters here after all the noise is removed and the important threads are brought together?
- What is the core insight when this entire information space is distilled?
- Where are the most important tensions or contradictions that synthesis must honestly reconcile?
- What would a decision-maker most need to understand from this research?

## Typical Deliverables
- Deep synthesis and insight distillation brief
- Key takeaway and pattern extraction report
- Multi-source evidence reconciliation and integrated analysis
- Research clarity and usability improvement
- Strategic synthesis for decision-maker use

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Theodora (research strategy), Verity (QA and bias challenge), Maialen (source reliability)
- Cross-guild: works with Athena or Clara (Strategy) when research synthesis must directly inform major strategic decisions
- Cross-guild: works with Zinnia (Data) when quantitative and qualitative synthesis must be integrated into decision intelligence
- Escalates to Theodora when synthesis reveals a fundamental knowledge gap or confidence problem that requires research reframing
