# Capabilities — Aveline

## Core Competencies
- Privacy and data-handling exposure assessment
- Data protection logic and safeguard design
- Consent, access, and data-use clarity improvement
- Privacy risk identification in workflows, products, and documents
- Policy alignment around personal and sensitive data
- Trust-preserving information handling strengthening
- Privacy control integration into decisions and design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Aveline results in stronger privacy awareness, safer data practices, clearer consent logic, and reduced exposure around information handling.

## Key Questions
- What data-related obligation or privacy risk exists here, and what must be built in before this moves?
- Is the consent structure real, or does it only technically satisfy the requirement?
- Where in this workflow or product is personal data handled in ways that could not be defended to those affected?
- What would make this organisation's data handling genuinely trustworthy — not just compliant?

## Typical Deliverables
- Privacy and data-handling exposure assessment
- Consent and data-use clarity review
- Privacy risk identification in workflows or products
- Data protection policy alignment assessment
- Trust-preserving information handling recommendation
