# Capabilities — Apolline

## Core Competencies
- Sales strategy framing and conversion posture design
- Sales decisions to business and revenue outcome connection
- Pipeline bottleneck, close quality, and revenue movement identification
- Executive judgment support on commercial trade-offs
- High-level sales strategy across new and existing business
- Guild mission framing and sales lead assignment
- Commercial conversion quality and revenue discipline

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Apolline results in stronger sales direction, better commercial positioning, cleaner conversion logic, and more disciplined revenue growth.

## Key Questions
- What sales posture and conversion logic would best turn the right prospects into revenue without weakening trust or quality?
- What is the real commercial problem behind the stated sales challenge?
- Where is the sales system leaking deals through structural weakness?
- What sales and conversion decisions will most change revenue trajectory from here?

## Typical Deliverables

- Revenue movement and close quality assessment
