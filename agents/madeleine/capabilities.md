# Capabilities — Madeleine

## Core Competencies
- Dispute exposure and litigation-like risk assessment
- Liability containment and remedy posture strengthening
- Fallback protection improvement for hostile or broken scenarios
- Escalation, breach, and claims-readiness logic
- Dispute vulnerability identification in structural weaknesses
- Defence posture strengthening in risky commitments
- Resilient downside planning support

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Madeleine results in fewer unprotected liabilities, stronger remedy positions, and more intelligent preparation for adversarial outcomes.

## Key Questions
- If this relationship breaks down, where are we exposed, and what protection do we really have?
- Are our remedies real — or nominal?
- What would a counterparty exploit in this agreement if they chose to?
- Where are we assuming cooperation that we have not contractually secured?

## Typical Deliverables
- Dispute risk and liability exposure assessment
- Liability containment recommendation
- Fallback and remedy posture review
- Claims-readiness and breach preparation brief
- Adversarial scenario planning input
