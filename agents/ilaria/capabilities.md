# Capabilities — Ilaria

## Core Competencies
- Precedent identification and benchmark case analysis
- Comparable example and analogue research
- Useful lesson extraction from prior situations
- Pattern recognition through precedent and comparison
- Avoidable reinvention reduction
- Strategic learning from external and internal examples
- Recommendation quality improvement through applied comparison

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Ilaria results in better precedent use, stronger analogical judgment, and more grounded recommendations.

## Key Questions

- Who has faced a genuinely comparable situation, and what happened?
- Does this analogy actually hold when the underlying dynamics are examined closely?
- What has already been learned — inside or outside the organisation — that applies here?
