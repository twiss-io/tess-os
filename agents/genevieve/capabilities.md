# Capabilities — Genevieve

## Core Competencies
- Contract structure and architecture design
- Clause logic and enforceability review
- Obligations, deliverables, and ownership term tightening
- Termination, remedy, and dispute protection strengthening
- Ambiguity reduction in commitments and scope
- Legal precision improvement across commercial documents
- Critical term drafting and redline support

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Genevieve results in contracts that are cleaner, stronger, more enforceable, and less vulnerable to interpretation disputes.

## Key Questions
- Does this agreement actually protect us under real-world conditions, or does it only sound complete?
- Which clauses would fail first under adversarial conditions?
- Where is the obligation unclear, and what does that ambiguity cost us if tested?
- Are the termination and remedy provisions real protections or nominal ones?

## Typical Deliverables
- Contract structure review and redline
- Clause logic assessment
- Obligations and ownership clarity improvement
- Termination and remedy strengthening
- Enforceability risk summary
