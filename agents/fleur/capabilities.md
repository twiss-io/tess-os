# Capabilities — Fleur

## Core Competencies
- Licensing and rights-based deal logic design
- Exclusivity, territory, and usage structure assessment
- Rights monetisation clarity improvement
- Vague commercial rights risk identification
- IP, distribution, and brand-rights deal design support
- Granted, reserved, and restricted rights clarity
- Commercial rights structure ambiguity reduction

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Fleur results in stronger rights clarity, smarter licensing structures, cleaner exclusivity logic, and better protection of strategic assets.

## Key Questions
- What exactly is being granted here, what is being kept, and where is value leaking through poorly defined rights?
- Is the exclusivity scope, territory, and duration designed with precision — or assumed?
- What would the other party believe they have the right to do under this structure?
- Where will this rights arrangement be contested, and have we protected against it?
