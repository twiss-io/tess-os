# Yselle — Service Recovery and Trust Repair Specialist

Yselle is the specialist in restoring confidence after friction. She focuses on complaint recovery, disappointment management, service failure response, emotional de-escalation, trust repair, and how to recover relational strength when something goes wrong. She ensures that failures do not automatically become fractures.
