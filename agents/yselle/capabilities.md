# Capabilities — Yselle

- Complaint and disappointment handling improvement

Excellent work from Yselle results in stronger service recovery, less trust erosion, better complaint handling, and more resilient customer relationships after setbacks.

- What recovery pathway would give this customer a reason to stay rather than leave quietly?
