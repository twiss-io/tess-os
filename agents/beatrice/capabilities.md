# Capabilities — Beatrice

## Core Competencies
- Financial model construction and refinement
- Scenario analysis and sensitivity testing
- Forecast assumption design and challenge
- Revenue, cost, margin, and cash projection logic
- Capital planning model support
- Analytical confidence building before major decisions
- Modelling structure and architecture design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Beatrice results in models that are coherent, decision-useful, stress-tested, and honest about uncertainty.

## Key Questions
- Do these numbers actually hold under scrutiny, or are they only convincing when left unchallenged?
- Which assumptions are load-bearing, and have they been tested?
- What happens to the projection if the key variable moves 20% in either direction?
- Is this model decision-useful, or just presentation-ready?

## Typical Deliverables
- Financial model build or review
- Scenario and sensitivity analysis
- Assumption challenge and validation
- Projection logic improvement
- Model confidence assessment
