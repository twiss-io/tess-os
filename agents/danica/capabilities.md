# Capabilities — Danica

## Core Competencies
- Analytics framing for major missions
- Measurement-to-business-question connection
- Most decision-useful metrics and signal identification
- Overall analytics posture and interpretation guidance
- Evidence-based leadership judgment support
- Misleading or incomplete measurement challenge
- High-level intelligence priority shaping across functions

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Danica results in cleaner measurement logic, better executive insight, stronger interpretation, and more evidence-based decision-making.

## Key Questions
- What should we actually be measuring and interpreting here if we want better decisions rather than more numbers?
- What is the analytical question beneath this request, and is it the right one?
- Where is the current measurement misleading rather than illuminating?
- What would better signal actually change about the decisions being made?

## Typical Deliverables
- Analytics framing brief
- Measurement priority recommendation
- Misleading metric identification and challenge
- Evidence-based decision support summary
- Intelligence posture assessment

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Guild anchor — leads or co-leads most high-stakes analytics and decision intelligence missions
- Works closely with: Zinnia (decision synthesis), Clarisse (KPI design), Linnea (BI architecture), Noemi (data integrity)
- Cross-guild: escalates to Tess when analytics findings materially affect strategic direction or business decisions
