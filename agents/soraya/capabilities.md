# Capabilities — Soraya

## Core Competencies
- Customer and user behavior pattern analysis
- Engagement, drop-off, and conversion behavior identification
- Segmentation and cohort-level insight surfacing
- Customer journey signal interpretation
- Data-to-user-reality connection
- Hidden friction and latent opportunity identification through behavior
- User-aware decision-making strengthening across functions

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Soraya results in better customer understanding, more useful segmentation insight, clearer behavioral interpretation, and smarter decisions across product and growth.

## Key Questions
- What are people actually doing, where are they dropping, and what does that reveal about the real experience?
- What does this segment's behavior tell us that the aggregate is hiding?
- Why would a rational user stop at this point — and what does that imply for the product or experience?
- Where is latent opportunity hidden in how users currently behave?

## Typical Deliverables
- Customer behavior analysis
- Drop-off and engagement interpretation
- Segmentation and cohort insight report
- Customer journey signal assessment
- Behavioral opportunity and friction identification
