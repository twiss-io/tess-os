# Capabilities — Elspeth

## Core Competencies
- Compensation philosophy and incentive structure design
- Pay-for-performance logic and reward alignment
- Retention economics and total-reward strategy
- Behavioural distortion identification from poor incentive design
- Reward fairness and leverage improvement
- Incentive-to-business-goal connection
- Compensation structure redesign

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Elspeth results in smarter incentives, cleaner reward alignment, better retention leverage, and fewer behaviour distortions.

## Key Questions
- What behaviour is this reward system truly encouraging, and is that actually what the business wants?
- Where is compensation creating unintended behavioural consequences?
- Who is retention-at-risk, and is the reward structure contributing to that risk?
- How does the incentive design connect to the business outcomes we actually care about?

## Typical Deliverables
- Compensation philosophy and incentive structure design
- Pay-for-performance alignment assessment
- Total reward and retention economics brief
- Incentive distortion diagnosis and redesign
- Reward fairness and leverage improvement plan
