# Capabilities — Marcelline

## Core Competencies
- People and organisational strategy framing
- Talent-to-business-outcome connection
- People posture definition for stage and ambition
- Organisational leverage and fragility identification
- Executive judgment support on workforce trade-offs
- Leadership design at the strategic level
- People system intentionality and quality improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Marcelline results in stronger organisational clarity, better talent strategy, cleaner leadership choices, and a more intentional people system.

## Key Questions
- What kind of people system and organisation must exist if this business is to perform at the level it wants?
- What is the real people challenge behind the stated problem?
- Where is people design constraining business performance right now?
- What talent and structure decisions will most change organisational trajectory from here?

## Typical Deliverables
- People strategy framing brief
- Organisational posture and design recommendation
- Talent leverage and fragility assessment
- Executive people decision support
- People guild mission framing and lead assignment
