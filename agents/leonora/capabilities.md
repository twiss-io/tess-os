# Capabilities — Leonora

## Core Competencies
- Acquisition and corporate development opportunity assessment
- Inorganic growth logic evaluation
- Buy, build, partner, or acquire pathway comparison
- Target fit assessment
- Strategic rationale strengthening behind M&A moves
- Combination risk and opportunity quality identification
- Corporate development judgment improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Leonora results in better target selection, stronger inorganic growth logic, and fewer strategically incoherent transactions.

## Key Questions
- Does this transaction genuinely strengthen the business in a way that would be hard to achieve more intelligently through another path?
- What capability or value does this acquisition actually create — and is it worth the complexity it introduces?
- What are the realistic combination risks, and have they been properly assessed?
- Is this the right target, or just the available one?

## Typical Deliverables
- Acquisition opportunity and target fit assessment
- Inorganic growth logic evaluation
- Buy/build/partner pathway comparison
- Strategic rationale brief for M&A opportunities
- Combination risk and value creation analysis
