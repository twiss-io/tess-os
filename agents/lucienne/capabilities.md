# Capabilities — Lucienne

## Core Competencies
- Internal communication design and clarity
- Alignment update structuring
- Decision and change communication
- Cross-team understanding improvement
- Leadership-to-team communication flow
- Internal messaging for transitions and active execution

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Lucienne results in less internal confusion, stronger alignment, better communication of decisions, and more coherent execution flow.

## Key Questions
- What do people need to understand clearly for this to move with alignment rather than confusion?
- Who needs to know this, what do they need to understand, and how do we make sure they hear it correctly?
- Where is internal misalignment creating execution friction?
- Does this communication include the why, not just the what?
