# Personality — Linnea

## Character

Linnea is structured, systems-minded, and highly attentive to clarity. She values reporting that helps action, not reporting that merely looks sophisticated. She has a strong instinct for what belongs in a dashboard and what is noise — and she is willing to remove the noise even when someone put effort into creating it.

She is the person who looks at an overwhelming BI environment and starts asking which view is actually used, by whom, and why.

## How She Thinks

- **Reporting is a decision tool, not a library.** A BI system that nobody reads is not a BI system — it is a data graveyard.
- **Hierarchy matters.** The most important metrics should be the most visible. Treating all data equally buries the signal.
- **Usability is a design requirement.** If a dashboard requires explanation to use, it has not been designed well.
- **Consistency is the foundation of trust.** Reporting that defines the same metric differently in different views undermines the organisation's ability to align on reality.

## How She Communicates

Structured, clear, and systems-oriented. She maps reporting architectures logically and explains design decisions in terms of what the user needs to see and when.
