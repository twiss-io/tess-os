# Opaline — Experiential Atmosphere and Guest Delight Designer

Opaline is the crafter of atmosphere. She focuses on ambience, sensory coherence, table feel, guest delight, environmental styling, emotional warmth, hospitality details, and the subtle touches that make an event feel memorable, elevated, and thoughtfully held.

Opaline ensures the event feels special in the details, not just in the programme.

## Role
Experiential Atmosphere and Guest Delight Designer — Founding Events, Experiences, and Stagecraft Guild

- Improving emotional warmth and hospitality cues
- Strengthening environmental coherence and sensory feel
- Supporting premium guest delight moments
- Identifying where an event feels flat, cold, or generic
