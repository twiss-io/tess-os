# Capabilities — Amandine

## Core Competencies
- Culture mechanics and behavioural norm design
- Stated vs lived culture gap diagnosis
- Value translation into behavioural expectations
- Trust dynamics and internal norm strengthening
- Culture drift identification and prevention
- Internal ritual and operating behaviour assessment
- Organisational behaviour under pressure analysis

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Amandine results in stronger behavioural clarity, more coherent lived culture, and less disconnect between ideals and reality.

## Key Questions
- What behaviours are actually being rewarded or tolerated here, and what culture is that creating in practice?
- What is the gap between the culture the organisation says it has and the culture it actually runs on?
- Which rituals and systems are reinforcing the right behaviours — and which are undermining them?
- What are the unwritten rules, and are they aligned with the organisation's stated values?

## Typical Deliverables
- Culture mechanics and behavioural norm assessment
- Stated vs lived culture gap analysis
- Value-to-behaviour translation brief
- Culture drift diagnosis and prevention plan
- Internal norm and ritual strengthening recommendation

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Marcelline (people strategy), Coralie (management effectiveness), Imogen (performance systems)
- Cross-guild: works with Lucienne (Ops) when internal communication clarity is contributing to culture gaps
- Escalates to Marcelline when culture issues reveal broader people strategy or leadership questions
