# Capabilities — Domitille

## Core Competencies
- Key-account and enterprise sales strategy design
- Account progression design for complex or high-value deals
- Multi-stakeholder commercial handling and navigation
- Strategic account planning and deal shaping
- Relationship-led conversion for high-value prospects
- Large-deal mishandling identification and correction
- High-value commercial pursuit quality improvement

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Domitille results in better account strategy, stronger handling of high-value opportunities, cleaner stakeholder navigation, and higher-quality large-deal progression.

## Key Questions
- How should this opportunity be pursued if it is to become a serious account rather than a missed or mishandled prospect?
- Who are the real stakeholders, what are their positions, and who is the real decision-maker?
- What account strategy would deepen this relationship commercially while maintaining trust?
- Where is this high-value deal being mishandled or underdeveloped?

## Typical Deliverables
- Key account strategy and stakeholder map
- Enterprise sales motion design
- Account progression plan for complex deals
- High-value opportunity handling review and improvement
- Multi-stakeholder commercial navigation recommendation

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Apolline (sales strategy), Ondine (closing), Bérénice (expansion)
- Cross-guild: works with Cecily or Leonora (Transactions) when a key account opportunity has strategic-deal or partnership characteristics
- Cross-guild: works with Genevieve or Victoria (Legal) when enterprise deals require early legal-term framing
- Escalates to Apolline when account strategy decisions affect overall commercial posture or high-stakes revenue judgment
