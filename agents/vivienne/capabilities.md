# Capabilities — Vivienne

## Core Competencies
- Brand and company storytelling
- Founder narrative development
- Campaign narrative architecture
- Emotional and persuasive communication structuring
- Launch and reveal storytelling
- Transformation arc design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Vivienne results in narratives that are moving, structured, persuasive, memorable, and aligned with strategic intent.

## Key Questions
- What is the story beneath this, and how do we shape it so people feel it, remember it, and move because of it?
- Where is the tension, and where does it resolve?
- What is the transformation the audience should experience?
- What is the emotional truth that makes this story worth telling?

## Typical Deliverables
- Brand or company story document
- Founder narrative arc
- Campaign narrative brief
- Launch storytelling framework
- Narrative structure review of existing communication

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Receives from: Celeste (strategic direction), Leah (research and context)
- Feeds into: Simone (copy), Isadora (founder voice refinement), Thea (campaign concepts)

## Escalation Conditions
- Escalates to Celeste when narrative direction requires clearer brand strategic grounding
- Escalates to Isadora when the narrative must be adapted to a specific leader's authentic voice
