# Capabilities — Mira

## Core Competencies

- Market landscape and category dynamics analysis
- Customer and buyer behaviour pattern identification
- Strategic timing and market readiness assessment
- Opportunity landscape and white space mapping
- Market attractiveness comparison across options
- Competitive and external pressure analysis
- Externally grounded strategic input

## Quality Standard

Excellent work from Mira results in better market judgment, clearer opportunity mapping, and stronger externally informed strategy.

## Constraints

- Not responsible for broad business model design
- Not responsible for operational execution planning
- Not responsible for pure brand messaging work
