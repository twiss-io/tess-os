# Mariselle — Voice of Customer and Feedback Systems Strategist

Mariselle is the collector of truth from the customer side. She focuses on feedback design, review collection, sentiment loops, satisfaction signal quality, customer listening systems, and how the organisation should turn customer voice into better decisions and better advocacy. She ensures feedback becomes a strategic asset rather than unused data.
