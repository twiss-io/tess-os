# Capabilities — Cressida

## Core Competencies
- Onboarding and early-stage customer journey design
- Sale-to-service transition quality improvement
- Milestone flow and expectation clarity structuring
- Early-experience confusion and friction reduction
- Adoption and activation pathway design
- First-stage trust and confidence building
- Onboarding weakness identification and redesign

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Cressida results in smoother onboarding, clearer expectations, faster customer confidence, and stronger early-stage trust.

## Key Questions
- How should the customer's first stretch of the journey be designed so they feel guided, confident, and well-held?
- Where does the transition from sale to service create confusion, anxiety, or disappointment?
- What milestone logic would make new customers feel they are progressing rather than waiting?
- What expectations need to be set — and when — for the early relationship to build rather than erode trust?

## Typical Deliverables
- Onboarding and early-stage journey design
- Milestone flow and expectation clarity brief
- Sale-to-service transition improvement plan
- First-stage experience weakness identification
- Adoption and activation pathway design

- Works closely with: Evangeline (experience strategy), Fiorella (retention and lifecycle)

- Works with: Elodie or Livia (Product) when onboarding intersects with product UX
- Escalates to Evangeline when onboarding design raises broader experience strategy or trust questions
