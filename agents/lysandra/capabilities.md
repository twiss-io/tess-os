# Capabilities — Lysandra

## Core Competencies
- Creative quality and coherence review
- Taste-level critique and standard pressure-testing
- Visual quality control and pre-release review
- Design inconsistency and dilution identification
- Premium standard maintenance across outputs
- Launch confidence improvement through honest critique
- Creative quality control framework

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Lysandra results in stronger design confidence, better visual discipline, fewer weak executions, and more consistent premium quality.

## Key Questions
- Is this truly good enough to represent the brand, or are we accepting something that only looks acceptable because we are too close to it?
- What is specifically weak about this execution, and what would make it genuinely stronger?
- Where has proximity to the work lowered the standard below what the brand actually requires?
- What would a demanding external creative eye see in this work that the team has stopped noticing?

## Typical Deliverables
- Creative quality review and critique
- Pre-launch visual standard assessment
- Design coherence and taste-level audit
- Specific improvement recommendations before release
- Creative quality confidence report

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Lavinia (creative strategy), and all guild specialists based on what is under review
- Cross-guild: works with Noelle (Brand) when verbal and visual quality must be reviewed together as a unified output
- Cross-guild: works with Anaïs (Product) when creative and product quality must be reviewed jointly before launch
- Escalates to Lavinia when quality review findings suggest the execution requires fundamental creative redirection
