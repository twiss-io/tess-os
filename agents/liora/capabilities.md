# Capabilities — Liora

## Core Competencies
- Audience journey and guest flow design
- Arrival experience and first-impression architecture
- Transition, seating logic, and wayfinding improvement
- Friction identification in event movement and navigation
- Experiential continuity and audience comfort design
- Flow alignment between physical movement and emotional journey
- Guest experience audit from entry to exit

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Liora results in smoother guest movement, less friction, better audience comfort, and a more coherent experience.

## Key Question
"How should guests move through this event so it feels smooth, intentional, and confidence-building at every step?"

## Typical Deliverables
- Guest journey map and flow architecture
- Arrival and transition quality improvement plan
- Friction and confusion point audit
- Wayfinding and navigation recommendations
- Audience comfort and experiential continuity review

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Zéphirine (event strategy), Opaline (atmosphere and guest delight), Virelai (stage sequencing alignment)
- Cross-guild: works with Callista (CX) when premium guest experience design is central to the event
- Escalates to Zéphirine when audience flow problems point to deeper event strategy or posture issues
