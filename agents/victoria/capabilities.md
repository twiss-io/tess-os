# Capabilities — Victoria

## Core Competencies
- Legal and risk framing for complex or high-stakes missions
- Major exposure identification and protection prioritisation
- Legal posture and structural safeguards guidance
- Downside risk assessment across agreements and decisions
- Legal-to-business strategic translation
- Leadership judgment support on legal trade-offs
- High-level governance and protection logic design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Victoria results in stronger protection, clearer legal posture, better risk awareness, and smarter structuring of commitments and safeguards.

## Key Questions
- What must be protected here, and what legal posture best supports the business without creating unnecessary drag?
- Where does the real risk sit — and is it currently visible to the people making decisions?
- What is the difference between what this agreement says and what it will mean under stress?
- What structural safeguards must be in place before this commitment is made?

## Typical Deliverables
- Legal and risk framing brief
- Major exposure summary
- Legal posture recommendation
- Structural protection assessment
- Governance and safeguard design input
- Trade-off analysis for high-stakes legal decisions

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Guild anchor — leads or co-leads most high-stakes legal and risk missions
- Works closely with: Seraphine (risk mapping), Genevieve (contract architecture), Sabine (compliance), Madeleine (dispute/liability)
- Cross-guild: escalates to Tess when a legal mission materially affects strategic direction or leadership decisions
- Paired with strategy guild when legal posture must align with commercial or competitive strategy
