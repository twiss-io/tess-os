# Capabilities — Bérénice

## Core Competencies
- Renewal and account expansion strategy design
- Upsell and cross-sell pathway improvement
- Account growth planning for existing customers
- Revenue leakage from weak renewal handling reduction
- Commercial continuity after the initial close strengthening
- Expansion opportunity quality improvement within existing relationships
- Customer success and sales expansion connection

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Bérénice results in stronger renewals, better account growth, cleaner expansion logic, and more durable revenue from existing relationships.

## Key Questions
- How do we deepen this account commercially in a way that feels timely, valuable, and earned?
- What expansion opportunity is this customer ready for, and how do we approach it without feeling extractive?
- Where is renewal risk building quietly before it becomes a lost account?
- What upsell or cross-sell logic connects most directly to this customer's actual outcomes?

## Typical Deliverables
- Renewal strategy and risk assessment
- Account expansion and upsell pathway design
- Customer revenue growth planning framework
- Expansion readiness and timing recommendation
- Account growth quality and commercial continuity improvement plan
