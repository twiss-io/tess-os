# Capabilities — Tamsin

## Core Competencies
- Competitive and market landscape mapping
- Market, category, and external player dynamic analysis
- Strategic pattern identification across the landscape
- Decision-making through external context support
- Company-relative competitive positioning clarity
- Ecosystem shift and market pressure point surfacing
- Outward-looking intelligence strengthening

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Tamsin results in clearer landscape understanding, stronger competitive context, and better externally grounded judgment.

## Key Questions
- What does the broader landscape actually look like, and what does that imply for the decision in front of us?
- Where are the real competitive pressures, structural dynamics, and ecosystem shifts that matter here?
- How does our position compare to others in this landscape, and what does that tell us?
- What is the landscape doing that our internal strategy has not yet accounted for?

## Typical Deliverables
- Competitive landscape map and analysis
- Market and category dynamic assessment
- Ecosystem shift and structural pressure identification
- Competitive positioning clarity report
- Landscape intelligence brief for strategic decision support

## Collaboration Dependencies
- Works closely with: Theodora (research strategy), Ilaria (precedent and analogues), Mélisande (synthesis)
- Cross-guild: works with Mira (Strategy) when landscape research must connect directly to market intelligence and strategic positioning
- Cross-guild: works with Sienna (Strategy) when competitive landscape clarity must inform differentiation and positioning decisions
- Escalates to Theodora when landscape findings materially change the strategic picture or research confidence
