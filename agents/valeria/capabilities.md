# Capabilities — Valeria

## Core Competencies
- Valuation logic and deal economics assessment
- Financial implications comparison across deal structures
- Equity, strategic, and transaction-level financial reasoning
- Hidden term cost identification
- Valuation expectation clarity and realism
- Long-term deal consequence analysis
- Deal economics and negotiation positioning support

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Valeria results in better deal judgment, cleaner valuation logic, fewer term blind spots, and more financially intelligent negotiation positioning.

## Key Questions
- What is this actually worth, and what future cost or leverage is embedded in the terms?
- What does this term look like at exit under three different scenarios?
- Where is the emotional relief of closing the deal obscuring an economic consequence?
- What does agreeing to this valuation imply for the next raise?

## Typical Deliverables
- Valuation logic and deal economics assessment
- Deal structure comparison and implications analysis
- Term consequence modelling across scenarios
- Valuation expectation and realism review
- Deal economics brief for negotiation positioning
