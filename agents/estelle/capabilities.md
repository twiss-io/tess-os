# Capabilities — Estelle

## Core Competencies
- Unit economics assessment and profitability logic
- Margin structure and contribution quality evaluation
- Acquisition, pricing, and cost connection to financial health
- Economic fragility identification beneath growth narratives
- Scale decision discipline support
- Profitability awareness improvement in product and revenue choices
- Economic quality of growth analysis

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Estelle results in clearer unit economics, stronger profitability insight, and more honest understanding of what scale is actually worth.

## Key Questions
- Does this growth actually improve the business economically, or does it simply make the top line bigger?
- What is the real contribution margin, and is it improving or deteriorating as we scale?
- Are CAC and LTV moving in the right direction relative to each other?
- What does the gross margin structure reveal about the long-term viability of this model?

## Typical Deliverables
- Unit economics assessment
- Contribution margin and profitability analysis
- CAC/LTV interplay review
- Economic quality of growth evaluation
- Margin structure and operating leverage assessment
