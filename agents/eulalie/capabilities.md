# Capabilities — Eulalie

## Core Competencies
- Premium touchpoint and experience moment design
- Packaging, presentation, and styling quality improvement
- Emotional and sensory experience design
- Flat or underwhelming experience detail identification
- Customer-facing premium expression alignment
- Perceived value and touchpoint design alignment
- Memorability through detail, restraint, and craft

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Eulalie results in more premium-feeling touchpoints, better experiential detail, stronger emotional polish, and clearer value perception.

## Key Questions
- What details would make this moment feel truly considered, elevated, and worth remembering?
- Where does the experience feel functional but not thoughtful?
- What touchpoint details are quietly eroding perceived premium value?
- What would a world-class design studio do differently with this moment?

## Typical Deliverables
- Premium touchpoint styling brief and recommendation
- Packaging and presentation design direction
- Experience moment design critique and improvement
- Sensory and emotional detail audit
- Premium expression alignment recommendation
