# Capabilities — Gaïane

## Core Competencies
- Consultative selling and needs diagnosis improvement
- Trust-based conversion logic design
- Weak-fit close and mis-sold outcome reduction
- Prospect need and offer fit alignment
- Educational and persuasive sales flow design
- Buyer understanding and conversion quality improvement
- Trust-sensitive sales conversation guidance

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Gaïane results in stronger consultative sales flow, better buyer fit, fewer poor closes, and more credible conversion.

- What does this buyer truly need to understand, believe, and feel before the right decision becomes easy to make?
- What is this buyer actually trying to solve, and is our offer genuinely the right answer?
- Where is the sales conversation producing commitment without producing understanding?
- What would make this conversion feel inevitable rather than manufactured to the buyer?
