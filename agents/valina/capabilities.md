# Capabilities — Valina

- Feature structure and system relationship definition
- Product modularity and coherence improvement
- Feature interaction and connection design
- Scope design and product logic shaping
- Redundant or conflicting feature reduction
- Product system clarity strengthening

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Valina results in cleaner feature logic, stronger modularity, less redundancy, and more scalable product design.
