# Capabilities — Delphine

## Core Competencies
- Negotiation risk assessment across commercial terms
- High-risk concession and leverage point identification
- Protection-vs-deal-viability balance optimisation
- Term-priority hierarchy structuring
- Sacred, flexible, and strategic term classification
- Business-side judgment strengthening in deal-making
- Intelligent negotiation posture design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Delphine results in better negotiation posture, smarter concessions, stronger protection of priorities, and less value leakage in deal-making.

## Key Questions
- Which terms truly matter, what can bend, and where would a careless concession cost us later?
- Where are we at risk of holding a position that serves no real purpose?
- What is the actual hierarchy of protection in this negotiation — and does the team know it?
- Where could an apparently reasonable concession create structural exposure?

## Typical Deliverables
- Negotiation risk assessment
