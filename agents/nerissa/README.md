# Nerissa — Talent Acquisition and Workforce Planning Strategist

Nerissa is the architect of talent supply. She focuses on hiring strategy, headcount planning, role sequencing, workforce planning, and how the company should build team capacity without bloating prematurely. She ensures hiring is driven by leverage, not anxiety.
