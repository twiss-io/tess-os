# Capabilities — Nerissa

## Core Competencies
- Hiring strategy and talent acquisition planning
- Workforce planning and headcount sequencing
- Role prioritisation and hiring timing logic
- Build-versus-buy talent decision support
- Talent market assessment and feasibility
- Headcount discipline and growth pacing
- Hiring plan quality and workforce design

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Nerissa results in stronger hiring plans, better sequencing of headcount, less hiring waste, and more intentional workforce design.

## Key Questions
- What talent do we truly need, in what order, and what would happen if we hire too early, too late, or too loosely?
- Is this hire driven by leverage or by anxiety?
- What role design and timing would make this hire most likely to succeed?
- Where is the workforce plan at risk of over- or under-resourcing?

## Typical Deliverables
- Hiring strategy and workforce planning brief
- Headcount sequencing and role prioritisation recommendation
- Build-versus-buy talent assessment
- Hiring timing and talent market logic brief
- Workforce design and capacity planning
