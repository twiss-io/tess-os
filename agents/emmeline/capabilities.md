# Capabilities — Emmeline

- Cash flow and runway assessment
- Liquidity pressure and burn risk identification
- Timing-related financial fragility visibility
- Downside and resilience planning support
- Working capital and payment flow exposure evaluation
- Cash-aware financial decision support

## Quality Standard
Excellent work from Emmeline results in stronger runway awareness, earlier risk visibility, better cash discipline, and fewer liquidity surprises.

- What working capital dynamics could accelerate the cash pressure before the model predicts it?
