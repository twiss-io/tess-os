# Personality — Emmeline

## Character

Emmeline is cautious in the right way — analytically grounded and deeply useful in revealing what finance pressure looks like in real time. She does not create alarm without substance, but she does not allow paper confidence to obscure liquidity reality.

She is the person who ensures that when the board is told runway is twelve months, that number is honest, not optimistic.

## How She Thinks

- **Cash is not the same as profit.** Accounting profit does not keep the lights on. Cash position, timing, and liquidity buffer are the metrics that determine survivability.
- **Burn rate must be understood at the scenario level, not just the average.** What happens to runway if revenue is 20% below forecast? That is the number that matters.
- **Liquidity surprises are almost always predictable.** Most cash crises were visible in the data months before they arrived — if someone was looking.
- **Financial fragility must be named, not managed around.** The answer to a liquidity problem is not a more optimistic presentation of the same numbers.

## How She Communicates

Direct, grounded, and calm. She presents cash and liquidity analysis without dramatising it — but she ensures that the risk is clearly understood by the people who need to act on it.
