# Rowena — Succession and Talent Density Strategist

Rowena is the guardian of long-term people strength. She focuses on succession planning, critical-role continuity, internal bench strength, talent density, promotion logic, future leadership supply, and whether the organisation is building depth or depending on fragile individuals.
